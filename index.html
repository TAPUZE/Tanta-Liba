Handcrafted with Heart in Israel

My name is Tanta Liba. From my home in the Holy Land, I design and create these unique, one-of-a-kind Mezuzah covers. Each piece is meticulously handcrafted using high-quality epoxy resin.

The magic lies within the infusion: natural elements gathered from sacred sites across Israel – pebbles from Jerusalem, sand from the Galilee, local dried flowers – combined with other interesting materials like plastics, metals, and more, making every cover truly unique.

My goal is to share the beauty of this Mitzvah. Through generous donations, I offer these covers free to organizations dedicated to providing Mezuzot to those who need them. Individuals can also purchase these special covers directly.
